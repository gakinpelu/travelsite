Bienvenue a Paris!

J'adore le ville lumiere!

I just cannot say enough about Paris, except for the fact that I have been there 5 different times. One time, I even went back to back within a six month time frame. 'Nuff said. From art to cuisine to fashion to technology, my advice to anywone who has yet to step oversears is to make Paris your first destination; so much bang for your buck in regards to stuff to see. Just don't go in the dead of winter or the heat of summer and you will be fine!
